# Nook — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 9 April 2026*

## Summary

nook does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

## Data Storage

nook uses your browser's local storage to save:

- Sticky note content, positions, and colours
- Decoration placements and scene configuration
- User preferences (theme, accent colour, sound settings, selected background)

This data is stored entirely on your device using the browser's built-in `localStorage` API. It is never sent to any server, third party, or external service.

## Data Collection

nook does **not** collect:

- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Analytics or usage data
- Cookies or tracking identifiers

## Third-Party Services

nook does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

## Permissions

nook requires the `newtab` override permission to replace your new tab page. No other browser permissions are used.

## Data Deletion

All nook data can be removed by:

1. Uninstalling the extension, or
2. Clearing your browser's local storage for the extension

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/joyceyeo/nook](https://github.com/joyceyeo/nook).

Any changes to this policy will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
